Generative AI is built on transformers, encoder-decoder architectures, and reinforcement learning.

Core concepts:

  • Transformers enable the model to learn language, logic, and structure.
  • GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks) pit two networks against each other‚Äîone generates, the other critiques.
  • Diffusion models refine noisy data into clear outputs.
  • RLHF (Reinforcement Learning with Human Feedback) ensures alignment with human values.
